ABOUT US

VIT Vellore

VIT University or VIT, formerly called Vellore Engineering College, is an Indian institute of higher education and a deemed university under Section 3 of the UGC Act. Founded in 1984, as Vellore Engineering College, by G. Viswanathan, the institution offers 20 undergraduate, 34 postgraduate, four integrated and four research programs. It has campuses in Vellore and Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Read MoreFoundation stone for VIT Bhopal campus at Sehore, Madhya Pradesh was laid down by Mr. Shivraj Singh Chauhan (Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh) on 20 October 2016.VIT has been ranked No. 1 private university and 13th overall best university ahead of many IITs and NITs in India, by National Institutional Ranking Framework published by Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India in April 2016. It has been ranked 4th best engineering college in India by the renowned IndiaToday Nielsen Survey 2016. It has been constantly ranked in the Top 10 Engineering colleges in India for the past several years by renowned rankings which include India Today Group, The Week, Hindustan Times, Career 360 and many more.

IETE-ISF

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is India's leading recognized professional society devoted to the advancement of Science and Technology of Electronics, Telecommunication and IT. The Institution provides leadership in Scientific and Technical areas of direct importance to the national development and economy. Read MoreGovernment of India has recognized IETE as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (SIRO) and also notified as an educational Institution of national eminence. The objectives of IETE focus on advancing electro-technology. The IETE conducts and partners technical meetings, conferences, symposia, and exhibitions all over India, publishes technical journals and provides continuing education as well as career advancement opportunities to its members.

SYNERGY

Synergy 2018 is an International Technical Symposium organized by Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE-ISF) in association with School of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering - VIT University, Vellore. Symposium offers a one-of-a-kind experience to students by providing an array of opportunities under one umbrella. Read MoreThe idea behind Synergy'18 is to train students in future forward technologies through workshops and provide global exposure and esoteric knowledge through guest lectures delivered by eminent personalities.Synergy'18 looks forward to promote; ”Envisioning the Future”, which not only embraces one's desire to become better, but also enables one to be able to explain the myriad of emotions a person experiences on the path to success and spread the word.

HackVit

HackVit is a 30-hour hackathon organized by IETE-ISF for innovators across the country. It is an great opportunity to put your technical skills to use. Apply your textbook knowledge to real life ideas and stand a chance to win exclusive goodies, schwags, certificates and cash prizes upto Rs. 20,000. Mark your calendars for 16th and 17th March 2019.

Code-Olymia

In thrilling 12-hours non-stop coding fest, participants will be given programming questions of varying levels, and there will be a plethora of coding languages to choose from, in order to solve them. This event will be held in association with HackerRank. All the participants will get participation certificates, and the top 5 teams will get exciting goodies, from HackerRank. Team of two allowed. Cash prizes worth 9000Rs.

REG FEE: 300/- per participantREGISTER

Paper Presentation

The Paper Presentation Event at Synergy'18 aims to provide the best platform to showcase your research ideas in front of an esteemed panel of judges. Mail your CSE/IT/ECE/EEE/EIE related abstracts to us and if your idea gets selected, it will be published in the International Journal for Research in Applied Science and Engineering Technology (IJRASET) with an impact factor of 6.887 and an ISRA journal impact factor of 3.166.

Registration Fee: - 500 / per participantREGISTER

Machine Learning

This two days long workshop will introduce the concepts of artificial neural networks (ANN), and present machine learning to the participants, through applications of image processing, data mining etc. Field experts from Scientific Computing Solutions, Chennai will take students through hand-worked numerical examples using the MATLAB & SciLAB softwares.

REG FEE: 900/- per participantREGISTER

Robotics

In this workshop spanning 2 days, participants will be brushed up with the basics of robotics and using the Arduino software. They will be taught how to handle the L293D IC and constructing a robot using it and will also be helped in building voice recognition Android app, controlling a robot using the app and much more! Hardware kits and a certificate will also be provided .

REG FEE: 900/- per participantREGISTER

VLSI

In this workshop, over the course of 2 days, you will be taught various topics like VLSI design methodologies and review of digital systems. Hands-on practical knowledge will be imparted as the participants will be taught design entry methods using Verilog HDL, FSM design and lots of other interesting things. This workshop will be conducted by trained professionals from the 'India Labs’.

REG FEE: 1000/- per participantREGISTER
